Boruto 28 Summary

Koji infiltrates the leaf village without being dictated while managing to leave behind Delta. Naruto invites Boruto for a sparring match. When Boruto loses, Kawaki suggests that he use Karma. He activates his own Karma to help Boruto with resonance. With Karma, all of Boruto's attacks are much more powerful. Naruto is impressed and says they should learn more about Karma. Kawaki reminisces how he was beaten up in his younger days by Jigen and tells Naruto he'd like to do the shadow clone technique so he can beat himself up. Naruto says he tried that himself ages ago and that it doesn't help. The chapter ends with Koji locating Kawaki.
