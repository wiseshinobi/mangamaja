One Piece 925 Summary

Perona reads the news about Gecko Moria. Mihawk advises her to go to Moria. Moria is upset that his crewmate, Absalom, who had the power to go invisible, has been killed and a member of the blackbear pirates has acquired that power. Blackbeard exclaims that the revolutionary army has attacked the Reverie to rescue Kuma, and that Kaido and the worst generation are in Wano with Big Mom hot on their tails. In Wano, Inurashi and Ashura fight and call a truce. Ashura reveals that he was a comrade of Oden and that he is very upset about the Samurai who abandoned him for 20 years. Tama has recovered from her encounter with Kaido and says that Umami, the horsewoman, fought fiercely for her sake. The chapter ends with Jack being ordered by "King" and "Queen" to get moving (to Wano?).
