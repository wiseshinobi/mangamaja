One Piece 922 Summary

Law reasons that Kaido has only come to know about Luffy's presence on the island and they don't have any idea about Kinemon's plan of overthrowing Orichi. Everyone is expecting Kaido to go on a rampage, but he seems to be drunk and says he'll forget the past if Shutenmaru joins him as a subordinate. Hawkins tells Kaido that Luffy and Law are in Oden castle. This snaps Kaido out of his drunkenness. Hawkins says he lied but it would still be a good idea to destroy Oden castle which is serving as a symbol of hope for the citizens who are expecting the legend of Orichi's destruction to come true. Kaido destroys Oden castle is just one shot! An enraged Luffy uses elephant gun and smacks Kaido on the head!
