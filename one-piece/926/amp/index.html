One Piece 926 Summary

The chapter begins with Kamazou, a bandit, receiving a chit with the crescent moon insignia. Ussopp, who is nearby, smiles indicating that the Kozuki family allies are back. More old allies of Oden get recruited. Nami and the shinobi are trying to eavesdrop and find out about an armament shipment. But they are spotted and make a run for it. With difficulty, they pass off the whole thing as a stray cat and manage to escape. Sanji is making delicious noodles to attract attention, but it seems to be mostly getting the ladies than any Samurai. Back in the prison, an old man gets very little food for his labor and is harrassed by the guards. Luffy and Kidd are able to do so much work despite the seastone cuffs that they end up earning a lot of food. The warden, Dobon, is angry that he doesn't have enough food left and beats the old man who barely got any food. Luffy notices this but continues to eat. Dobon threatens to discipline Luffy and Kidd for eating so much, but he is defeated in an instant despite the seastone cuffs.
